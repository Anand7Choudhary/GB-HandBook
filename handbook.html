VC Metrics Handbook

A comprehensive guide to evaluating early-stage startups in the Indian ecosystem

Executive Summary

This handbook provides a structured framework for evaluating early-stage startups in India, focusing on key metrics, industry benchmarks, and sector-specific considerations. Whether you're a founder looking to understand investor expectations or an investor seeking to make data-driven decisions, this guide will help you navigate the complex landscape of startup evaluation.

Key Takeaways

  • Understanding the fundamental metrics that matter in early-stage investing
  • Learning how to benchmark startups against industry standards
  • Identifying red flags and potential risks in startup evaluation
  • Developing a structured approach to due diligence
